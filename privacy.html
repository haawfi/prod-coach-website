Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Productivity Coach ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Productivity Coach mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Screen Time & Usage Data: The app collects information about your device usage patterns, including which apps you use and how long you use them. This data is used to generate insights, track progress toward your goals, and power the AI coaching features.

Goals & Preferences: When you set productivity goals, configure focus sessions, or interact with features, we store your preferences to personalize your experience.

AI Coaching Conversations: Messages you send to the AI Coach are transmitted to our backend servers to generate responses. Conversation history is used to provide contextual, personalized coaching.

Account Information: If you create an account or subscribe to premium features, we collect the information necessary to manage your subscription (processed securely through Google Play).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide screen time tracking and usage insights
  • Power the AI coaching experience with personalized advice
  • Generate daily briefings and micro-challenges
  • Track your goals and streaks
  • Improve the app's features and user experience

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the safety and security of our users
  • With service providers who assist in operating the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (see our Data Deletion page)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data in a portable format

7. Children's Privacy

Productivity Coach is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the app and maintain an active account. If you request data deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days of receiving the request. Locally stored data is removed when you uninstall the app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
